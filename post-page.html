Find Out For Yourself

In a celebration of knowledge, intellect, and determination, the students of Delhi Public School (DPS) Varanasi proved their mettle once again at the Legal Quiz 1.0 held at IMS Varanasi. This remarkable achievement not only highlights the academic prowess of our students but also serves as a testament to the school's commitment to fostering holistic education. The exhilarating victory of Aryan Pathak and Ritwek Agarwal, Grade 12 students, in this competitive event adds another feather to the illustrious cap of DPS Varanasi.

The Legal Quiz 1.0 held at IMS Varanasi provided a platform for young legal minds to exhibit their understanding of intricate legal concepts, critical thinking abilities, and quick wit.
