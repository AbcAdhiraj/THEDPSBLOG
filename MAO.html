Triumph in the Mental Aptitude Olympiad

Find Out For Yourself

In the ever-evolving landscape of education, the pursuit of excellence takes on many forms. Delhi Public School (DPS) Varanasi has once again taken a significant stride in this direction by achieving remarkable success in the Mental Aptitude Olympiad 2023. The achievements of its students not only showcase their intellectual prowess but also underscore the school's commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals.

The resounding success of DPS Varanasi's students in the Mental Aptitude Olympiad 2023 has added a new feather to the school's cap. Four outstanding individuals from Grade 8 Middle Wing, namely Ishan Kushwaha (Rank 2), Daiwik Mani (Rank 7), Maham Abrar (Rank 11), and Samridh Saraogi (Rank 15), have not only made their mark but have brought pride and honor to the institution.

Behind every remarkable achievement lies untiring dedication and unyielding determination. These students, fueled by their passion for learning and their pursuit of excellence, have invested countless hours in honing their mental acuity and problem-solving skills. Their journey is a testament to the fact that true success is the result of consistent effort, hard work, and a genuine love for knowledge.

DPS Varanasi's success in the Mental Aptitude Olympiad reflects the school's dedication to providing a nurturing and intellectually stimulating environment. The institution's commitment to academic rigor, innovative teaching methodologies, and holistic development has undoubtedly played a pivotal role in shaping these exceptional young minds.

The achievements of Ishan Kushwaha, Daiwik Mani, Maham Abrar, and Samridh Saraogi serve as a source of inspiration for their peers, teachers, and the entire school community. Their journey encourages others to set their goals high, embrace challenges, and strive for excellence in every endeavor they undertake.
